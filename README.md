Waveshare ESP32-S3 4.3" Touch LCD Desk Stand
A 3D printable desk stand/enclosure for the Waveshare ESP32-S3 4.3inch Touch LCD Development Board, designed for use as a Home Assistant scene controller.

Overview:
This stand provides a stable, angled mounting solution for your Waveshare display, perfect for desk placement where you need quick access to your Home Assistant controls. The design positions the touchscreen at an optimal viewing and interaction angle while keeping cables organized.

Hardware Requirements:
Waveshare ESP32-S3 4.3inch Touch LCD Development Board
https://www.waveshare.com/esp32-s3-touch-lcd-4.3b.htm


Recommended Pring Settings:
Layer height: 0.2mm
Infill: 100%
Supports: Likely required
Material: PLA or PETG
Display cutout should measure 111.30mm x 74mm
